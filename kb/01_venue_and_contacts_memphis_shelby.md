## Where you file in Memphis / Shelby County

If you live in Memphis or Shelby County and need a conservatorship, you usually file in **Shelby County Probate Court**.

Shelby County Probate Court  
140 Adams Avenue, Room 124  
Memphis, TN 38103  

Clerk’s Office hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00 am – 4:30 pm.  
Phone: (901) 222‑3750  
Fax: (901) 222‑3756  

This court handles things like:

- Conservatorships and guardianships.  
- Name changes, birth certificate corrections.  
- Judicial mental health hospitalizations.  
- Wills and estates.

For help with:

- Copies of court papers: email probate.copies@shelbycountytn.gov.  
- General questions about forms or where to file: call (901) 222‑3750 or email probatehelp@shelbycountytn.gov.

The clerk’s office can explain forms and process, but cannot give legal advice.
